A Taste of Italy: Fresh Pasta Primavera

Pasta Primavera: A Fresh & Flavorful Italian Delight

Pasta Primavera is a vibrant and refreshing dish that celebrates the flavors of fresh, seasonal vegetables. Traditionally made with al dente pasta and a mix of sautéed vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and peas, this dish offers a perfect balance of crunch and tenderness. Tossed in a light olive oil or creamy sauce, it's a healthier yet indulgent Italian classic that’s bursting with flavor. Whether you’re enjoying it as a side dish or a main course, Pasta Primavera is perfect for any occasion, especially during the warmer months when fresh veggies are in abundance.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (Spaghetti, Penne, or Fusilli)
  • Bell Peppers (sliced thinly)
  • Zucchini (sliced or cut into half-moons)
  • Cherry Tomatoes (halved)
  • Olive Oil (for sautéing)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  3. Add Garlic & Seasoning: Stir in minced garlic and cook for another minute. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine Pasta & Veggies: Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the sautéed vegetables. Toss to combine, adding a little more olive oil if needed.
  5. Finish & Serve: Stir in fresh basil and a squeeze of lemon juice. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.

Enjoy Your Pasta Primavera!

There you have it—a quick, healthy, and delicious Pasta Primavera that’s perfect for any day of the week. With its fresh veggies and light olive oil dressing, it’s a dish that’s as satisfying as it is colorful. Serve it up with a side of garlic bread, and you’ve got yourself a meal everyone will love. Bon appétit!
